About

The Indian music club, popularly called IMC, is amongst the most active clubs of the college. We are an extremely diversified club dedicated to the promotion of Hindustani classical/semi classical, Carnatic, Bollywood, light, folk, regional, and many other forms of music. We provide the ideal platform for exchange of musical ideas, appreciation of music and musical development. Anyone with an ear for good music is invited to be a part of our family. Our club includes not just singers and instrumentalists, but also lyricists, composers, sound mixers, etc. The IMC room is situated in the beautiful Athletics Ground ( Anuj’s Dream Village), on the first floor of its lone building. We possessa range of instruments and sound systems which can be used to by the members. The club remains open on all weekdays (except Sundays and official holidays) from 5 p.m. to 8 p.m.
